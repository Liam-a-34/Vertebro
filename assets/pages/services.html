Spine Related

We use non-invasive techniques, such as spinal manipulation and adjustments, to correct misalignments in the spine and improve its function. Chiropractic care can help with a variety of spine-related issues, such as back pain, neck pain, headaches, and sciatica. Chiropractors can also provide advice on exercise, posture, and lifestyle changes to promote spinal health and prevent future issues.

Back Pain

We are trained to diagnose and treat back pain using a variety of techniques, such as spinal manipulation, mobilization, and massage. By adjusting the spine, we can improve its alignment, reduce inflammation, and alleviate pressure on the nerves, which can lead to pain relief.

Headaches & Migraines

We can help with headaches and migraines by targeting the root cause of the pain, which is often related to tension or misalignment in the neck and spine. By adjusting the spine, we can improve its alignment and reduce tension in the muscles and nerves that may be contributing to the headache or migraine. Additionally, we can provide advice on posture and exercises to help prevent future headaches and migraines.

Neck Pain

Chiropractic care can be an effective treatment option for neck pain, as it targets the root cause of the pain, which is often related to misalignment or tension in the spine and surrounding muscles. We use a variety of techniques, such as spinal manipulation, mobilization, and soft tissue therapy, to improve the alignment of the spine and reduce tension in the muscles and nerves that may be contributing to the pain.

Sciatica

Chiropractic care can be a safe and effective treatment option for sciatica, which is a condition characterized by pain and discomfort that radiates from the lower back down to the legs. We can use spinal manipulation, mobilization, and soft tissue therapy to improve the alignment of the spine and reduce pressure on the sciatic nerve, which can help alleviate sciatica symptoms.

Scoliosis

Chiropractic care can be an effective treatment option for scoliosis, which is a condition characterized by a sideways curvature of the spine. We can use spinal adjustments, exercises, and other techniques to improve the alignment of the spine, reduce tension in the muscles and nerves surrounding the spine, and alleviate symptoms associated with scoliosis, such as pain and discomfort. While chiropractic care cannot cure scoliosis, it can help manage the condition and prevent it from worsening.

Other Services

Check out some of the other services we can provide.

Blood Testing

Blood tests can provide information on a range of factors, including blood cell counts, cholesterol levels, hormone levels, liver and kidney function, and immune system health. By analyzing blood test results, we and healthcare providers can make informed decisions about patient care, such as adjusting medications or treatment plans as needed. Additionally, blood tests can be used as a preventive measure, helping to identify potential health concerns before they become more serious.

Hormone Testing

Hormonal imbalances can contribute to a variety of symptoms, such as fatigue, weight gain, mood changes, and fertility issues. Hormone testing can provide insight into levels of various hormones, such as testosterone, estrogen, progesterone, and thyroid hormones, which can help healthcare providers diagnose and treat hormonal imbalances. By analyzing hormone test results, we and healthcare providers can develop personalized treatment plans to address hormonal imbalances, such as hormone replacement therapy or lifestyle modifications.

Nutrition Consultation

A nutrition consultation typically involves an assessment of the individual's current diet, lifestyle, and health goals, followed by personalized recommendations for dietary and lifestyle changes. A nutrition consultation may also involve education on proper nutrition and healthy eating habits, as well as recommendations for supplements and other dietary aids. It can be particularly beneficial for individuals with specific health concerns or chronic conditions, such as diabetes or heart disease, as well as those looking to improve athletic performance or manage weight.

X-ray Imaging and Diagnostics

X-ray imaging and diagnostics can be a valuable service in diagnosing and monitoring a wide range of medical conditions and injuries. X-ray imaging uses a small amount of radiation to create images of the internal structures of the body, such as bones, joints, and organs. These images can be used to identify fractures, dislocations, and other injuries, as well as diagnose conditions such as pneumonia, lung cancer, and arthritis. X-ray diagnostics can also be used to monitor the progress of treatments and to evaluate the success of surgical procedures.

MRI / CT Interpretation

Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) and Computed Tomography (CT) scans are advanced imaging technologies that provide detailed images of the body's internal structures, including soft tissues, organs, and bones. These images can be used to identify a variety of medical conditions, such as tumors, blood clots, and brain injuries. However, interpreting these images requires specialized training and expertise. With MRI / CT scans, we and healthcare providers can accurately diagnose and treat medical conditions and develop effective treatment plans.
