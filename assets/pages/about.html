Welcome to Vertebro in Nashville

Our chiropractic team is dedicated to helping you and your family achieve optimal health. Chiropractic is a healthcare profession that utilizes spinal alignment to relieve a variety of physical ailments, such as chronic back pain, neck pain, and muscle strain. By correcting the alignment of the spinal column, chiropractic care provides a safe and non-invasive approach to pain relief.

If you are unsure whether chiropractic care is right for you, our team at Vertebro is here to help. We invite you to schedule a consultation to discuss your individual needs and goals. Our chiropractors will work with you to develop a personalized program designed to meet your specific needs. Contact us today to learn more about how chiropractic care can benefit you.

Dr. Zack Farmer, a native of Nashville, Tennessee, has always been deeply rooted in the world of chiropractic care. He obtained his Bachelor's Degree in Kinesiology from The University of Tennessee before achieving academic excellence as a magna cum laude graduate from Life University, one of the country's top chiropractic schools.

From an early age, Dr. Zack has experienced the benefits of chiropractic adjustments, thanks to his upbringing in a chiropractic family. His profound fascination with the intricacies of the human body led him to develop a keen interest in subjects such as nutrition, pathology, and orthopedic diagnosis during his schooling. Driven by a desire to address underlying causes rather than merely treating symptoms, he pursued an in-depth understanding of how the body functions.

Within his practice, Dr. Zack finds great joy in connecting with patients and helping them optimize their chiropractic care experience. While his primary focus lies in spinal adjustments, he possesses specialized expertise in adjusting extremities, including shoulders, elbows, wrists, knees, and ankles. In addition to his chiropractic expertise, he exhibits a strong passion for nutrition and fitness. Dr. Zack takes pleasure in sharing his knowledge regarding stretching techniques, exercise routines, dietary considerations, and overall wellness practices.

By choosing Dr. Zack Farmer as your chiropractor, you can rest assured that you are placing your trust in a compassionate practitioner who is dedicated to providing personalized care and improving your well-being.
